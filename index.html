Instructions for Students

Payment of Fees

The institute fee can be paid through following schemes.
  1. By Draft: This scheme is only for:
    1. Students availing educational loans.
    2. Students receiving scholarships.
      All drafts must be made in favour of MNNIT Fee Account A/C No: 30981350206 payabale at ALLAHABAD
  2. By SBI-MOPS Scheme: This facility can be used to deposit Institute fee by following methods :
    1. Net Banking of any bank.
    2. Credit/Debit card of any bank.
    3. Offline at Sbi Branch (through challan generated by SBI MOPS).

Steps for Onine Registration

  1. Login to the portal using your registration number and password as entered by you during the registration process of last semester.You will be directed to your profile. Please see that your personal data is correct and upto date. If you find any discrepancy in the non editable fields contact to Dean Academics Office with proper proof.
  2. Click the Registeration button on right side of your home page. You will be redirected to registration portal where you have to perform the following steps in order to complete your online registration.
  3. The tab marked "Update Information" will now be activated. Fill in the details asked on the portal and submit the form.
  4. Now the Portal's "Select Slot" tab will be activated. Select a slot according to your convenience (Please refer to the link Registration Slots for information about all the slots). If slot is not available, contact the webteam immediately.
  5. The Button marked "Pay Fees" will now be activated. Select the mode of payment [Draft/Online]. If you select payment by SBI-MOPS, you will be redirected to the payment gateway of SBI.
  6. If you had selected Draft option in step 5 above, enter detail of draft you had made for semester fee.

Steps for DASA Candidates

  1. If you have submitted DASA fees through SWIFT MESSAGE, report to Dean Office with receipt of the payment for confirmation of the payment.
  2. If you have submitted DASA fees through Draft, deposit the DD in Accounts Section, get receipt and report to Dean [Academic] office.
  3. Report respective departments for physical registration in your alloted slots.

Steps for Physical Registration

  1. Report at M.P. Halls and submit the mess fees.
  2. Report for physical registration in your allotted slot in your respective departments.
  • Students are advised to reach the venue one hour prior to the starting time of their slot so as to complete all the formalities beforehand.
  • You must be present in Person during On-Campus Registration.
  • Proxy Registration is not allowed under any circumstances.
  • For any queries at any stage please mail to academics@mnnit.ac.in
